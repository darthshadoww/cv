The Great Masterchief

Master Chief Petty Officer John-117, or Masterchief , is a fictional character and the protagonist in the Halo multimedia franchise. The Master Chief is a towering supersoldier known as a "Spartan", raised and trained from childhood for combat. He is almost faceless and rarely seen without his green-colored armor and helmet. He is commonly referred to by his naval rank rather than his given birth name.
